# local-seo-toolkit

A practical, opinionated toolkit for local SEO work on real client sites. Built from a decade of local roll-ups in Sonoma, Napa, and Marin counties.

## What's in here

- **Schema templates** — LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList with sane defaults for service-area and brick-and-mortar businesses
- **Robots + sitemap patterns** — robots.txt and sitemap.xml conventions that don't silently strip 30% of your URLs (looking at you, Astro default)
- **GBP optimization checklists** — 40 fields to fill, in order of ranking impact
- **Citation network targets** — the 20 citations that move the needle for local service businesses
- **OTTO / BonsaiX recipes** — configuration patterns for AI-first optimization platforms

## Who this is for

Agency operators and in-house SEOs running local service businesses. Not a theoretical resource — everything here is what we actually ship on [Bonsai Marketing](https://bonsaimarketingcompany.com) client sites.
